Vitality Vibe: BMI and Personalized Diet Plan Website


Objective The primary goal of this project is to create a website that allows users to calculate their Body Mass Index (BMI) and receive a personalized diet plan tailored to their BMI. The focus of the site is to promote a healthier lifestyle by providing users with balanced nutrient intake recommendations and a proper diet plan that aligns with their fitness goals.

Built-in Languages Html CSS JavaScript Key Features Personalized Diet Plan User-Friendly Interface

Target Audience:


The website is designed for anyone looking to understand their BMI and receive personalized dietary advice. This includes individuals who are aiming to lose weight, gain weight, or maintain a healthy lifestyle. The platform caters to a wide audience, from fitness enthusiasts to those new to health and nutrition.

Fitness and Wellness Tips:


General advice on staying fit, including exercise routines, hydration tips, and mental well-being.

Encouragement and motivation to help users stay on track with their diet and fitness goals.



Personalized Diet Recommendations

Vitality Vibe offers tailored diet plans based on your BMI, ensuring that your nutritional needs align with your body type and health goals.



Accurate BMI Calculator

The website features a precise BMI calculator that assesses your body mass index, helping you understand your ideal weight range and guiding your dietary choices.



Expert-Approved Nutrition Plans

Using your BMI data, Vitality Vibe provides nutrition plans curated by health experts to promote balanced eating and support your journey toward optimal health.

Note: This project not only addresses the growing concern for health and fitness but also provides a practical tool that can make a positive impact on users' lives.
